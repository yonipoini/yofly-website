Cookie Policy

Understanding how we use tracking technologies to personalize your experience.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences and provide a smoother user experience.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic operation of our platform. They enable secure login, session management, and verification processes within the app environment.

Analytical Cookies

We use anonymized analytical cookies to understand how our community interacts with our features. These allow us to improve our services and identify the most popular tools for crew members.

Managing Your Preferences

You can adjust your browser settings to decline cookies at any time. However, please note that some essential features of YoFly Crew may not function correctly if cookies are disabled.

Third-Party Cookies

YoFly Crew does not use tracking cookies from third-party advertisers. Any cookies on our platform are strictly for service-level operations and first-party analytics.

Updates to This Policy

Our cookie policy is reviewed regularly and updated to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. Please check this page for any updates periodically.
