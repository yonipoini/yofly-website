Our Mission

YoFly Crew was built by pilots and flight attendants for pilots and flight attendants.

Why We Built YoFly

We've all been there: landing at a new airport, wondering where the crew lounge is, trying to find a safe way to our layover hotel, or just looking for a familiar face in a new city.

Until now, crew member life was disconnected. We relied on outdated forums, scattered chat groups, and half-remembered tips from fellow crew members. YoFly Crew changes that by bringing our entire community into one verified, secure, and real-time platform.

Built By Crew

YoFly Crew is built by active flight attendants and pilots who understand exactly what you need on a layover or at a crew base.

No Data Selling

We take your privacy and security seriously. Your data stays within the crew community, and we will never sell it to third parties.
