Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our top priority. We're here to protect the crew member.

Information We Collect

YoFly Crew collects information that is essential to verifying your identity as an crew member and providing you with our core services.

Verification Data

To join YoFly Crew, you must provide proof of employment (airline ID or crew badge). This information is used solely for verification purposes and is never shared or sold.

Usage Data

We collect anonymized usage data to help us improve the app and provide the best possible experience for our community. This includes interaction with features and anonymous alerting logs.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to verify your account, provide real-time alerting services, manage the Marketplace, and maintain the safety of the Vent Room.

No Third-Party Sharing

YoFly Crew does not share or sell your personal information to third-party advertisers or data brokers. Your professional data is secure within our crew-only environment.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. If you choose to delete your account, all personal verification metrics are wiped from our system.
